# User Manual
## Agriculture Equipment Rental System (AERS)

Welcome to the Agriculture Equipment Rental System! This guide will help you navigate and use the platform effectively.

### 1. Getting Started

#### Registration
1. Click on **Register** in the top navigation bar.
2. Select your role: **Farmer** or **Equipment Owner**.
3. Fill in your details (Full Name, Email, Password, Phone, Address).
4. Click **Create Account**.

#### Login
1. Click on **Login** in the top navigation bar.
2. Enter your registered key (Email) and Password.
3. Click **Login**.

---

### 2. For Farmers

#### Browsing Equipment
- Go to the **Home** page or click **Browse Equipment**.
- Use the search bar to find specific machinery (e.g., "Tractor", "Harvester").
- Filter results by category or price if available.

#### Booking Equipment
1. Click on an equipment card to view details.
2. Check the **Availability** status.
3. Select your **Start Date** and **End Date**.
4. The system will calculate the total cost and the required 10% advance.
5. Click **Request Booking**.
6. Follow the instructions to pay the advance (mock payment).

#### Managing Bookings
- Go to your **Dashboard**.
- View **My Bookings** to see the status of your requests (Pending, Approved, Rejected).
- Once approved, you can view the owner's contact details.

---

### 3. For Equipment Owners

#### Listing Equipment
1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click **Add New Equipment**.
3. Enter details:
   - Name & Description
   - Category
   - Price per day
   - Upload clear images
4. Click **Submit**.

#### Managing Requests
1. In your **Dashboard**, go to **Booking Requests**.
2. Review incoming requests from farmers.
3. Click **Approve** to accept a booking or **Reject** to decline.
4. Once approved, coordinate with the farmer for delivery/pickup.

---

### 4. For Administrators

#### Dashboard Overview
- Login with admin credentials.
- View platform statistics: Total Users, Total Equipment, Total Bookings.

#### Management
- **Manage Users**: View customer and owner lists. Deactivate accounts if necessary.
- **Manage Equipment**: Review listings. Remove inappropriate or duplicate items.
- **Monitor Bookings**: Oversee rental activities and resolving disputes.
